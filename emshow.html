THE EMINEM SHOW

The Eminem Show is the fourth studio album by American rapper Eminem, released on May 21, 2002 by Aftermath Entertainment, Shady Records, and Interscope Records.
Artist: Eminem
Release date: 21 May 2002
Awards: Grammy Award for Best Rap Album, more
Nominations: Grammy Award for Album of the Year, more
Genres: Hip hop music, Hardcore hip hop, Political hip hop
