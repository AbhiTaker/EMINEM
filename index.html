EMINEM

Marshall Bruce Mathers III, known professionally as Eminem, is an American rapper, record producer, and actor. Eminem is the best-selling artist of the 2000s in the United States.
Born: 17 October 1972 (age 44 years), Saint Joseph, Missouri, United States
Full name: Marshall Bruce Mathers III
Movies and TV shows: 8 Mile, Pauly Shore Is Dead, Eminem: E, Shady Talez, The Slim Shady Show
Spouse: Kimberly Anne Scott (m. 2006–2006), Kimberly Anne Scott (m. 1999–2001)
